The VISTA Lab

Welcome to the home of the Wandell Lab at Stanford University – principal investigator: Brian Wandell. The VISTA Lab uses neuroimaging methods (e.g., fMRI, DTI) and behavior (e.g., psychophysics) to study the human visual system. We are presently studying the visual pathways that contribute to reading development as well as making quantitative measurements of reorganization following injury or disease. Our work includes extensive software development of tools and simulations, and we collaborate extensively with groups in Neuroscience, Electrical Engineering, and Computer Science.
